Disclaimer

This calculator is a pacing aid, not an automated budget recommendation tool.

  • Best used for a single campaign, ad set, or tactic with a reasonably consistent CPM.
  • Blended client totals with multiple ad sets, tactics, or mixed CPMs may produce misleading results.
  • Yesterday's Effective CPM is based on recent delivery and may vary significantly day to day.
  • The planned/input CPM from a budget sheet may differ from actual recent delivery CPM.
  • Final budget changes should still use platform behavior and human judgment.
